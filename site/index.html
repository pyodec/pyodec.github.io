About pyodec.github.io

Obviously this site is hosted by github.io, along with the rest of the project. This site is built with Bootstrap, which makes use of Glyphicons font icons, and jQuery. Additionally, I am a fan of FontAwesome, and like to use their icons wherever possible. And the limited, depressing graphics on this site were produced in Google drive.

That's really about it. Yay sort-of open source stuff.
